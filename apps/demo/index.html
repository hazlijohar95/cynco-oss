Register

One account’s virtualized Register built from EntryStore.getRegisterRows over the generated workload. Scroll the fixed 600px window; click a row to select it. Group by interleaves period header rows into the virtual row space; Range selection enables shift-click ranges and meta/ctrl-click toggling.

Entry diff

EntryDiff compares two versions of a journal entry — the audit-trail view. This handcrafted pair changes the cash amount (old struck through → new), rewords the narration (word-level highlights), and adds a bank-fee posting (debit/added family).

Entry stream

EntryStream renders journal entries as they arrive from a ReadableStream — appends are batched to one DOM write per frame, the footer tracks the running count, and stick-to-bottom autoscroll releases the moment you scroll up.

Reconciliation

Reconciliation treats bank reconciliation like conflict resolution: statement lines left, book postings right, proposed matches as tinted pairs. Hover a pair to accept (✓) or reject (✗); accept everything matched and watch the difference figure. The bank fee needs a new entry; cheque 001232 is still outstanding.

Chart of accounts

AccountTree over the workload’s chart with rolled-up balances. Flatten merges single-child group chains into one row. Double-click a selected row (or press F2) to rename it inline — Enter commits, Escape cancels, blur commits. Drag any row onto a group to re-parent it (drag a selected row to move the whole selection); hovering a collapsed group mid-drag springs it open.

Ledger view

LedgerView stacking five account registers behind one shared virtualizer with sticky section headers. Selection is a single focused row across all sections. The scroll-to buttons run the critically-damped smooth-scroll engine (wheel/touch/keys cancel it); Shuffle sections exercises incremental setSections — instances and DOM are reused, and the scroll position stays anchored to what you were looking at.
